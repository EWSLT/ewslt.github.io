About the Workshop

The Evaluation Workshop on Speech and Language Technologies (EWSLT) focuses on advancing evaluation methodologies for modern speech and language systems. Recent progress in ASR, TTS, speech translation, and speech-language models has created new challenges for reliable benchmarking and evaluation.

EWSLT brings together researchers working on speech processing, natural language processing, and multimodal AI to discuss datasets, benchmarks, evaluation metrics, and shared tasks for emerging language technologies.

Venue: IIIT Hyderabad, India
Date: December 2026 (Tentative)
Mode: Hybrid (In-person & Remote)

Workshop Topics

We invite work on evaluation and benchmarking for speech and language technologies including:
  • Evaluation of Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)
  • Evaluation of Text-to-Speech (TTS)
  • Speech Translation (ST) evaluation
  • Spoken Language Translation (SLT)
  • Speech-to-Speech translation systems
  • Evaluation of Speech LLMs and multimodal LLMs
  • Evaluation metrics beyond WER and BLEU
  • Human vs automatic evaluation methods
  • Robustness and bias in speech systems
  • Low-resource and multilingual evaluation
  • Benchmark datasets and shared tasks
  • Evaluation of prosody, style, and expressive speech

Shared Challenges

EWSLT will host evaluation challenges in the following areas:
  • ASR Evaluation Challenge
  • TTS Naturalness and Prosody Evaluation
  • Speech Translation Benchmark
  • Speech LLM Evaluation Challenge

Details about datasets, baseline models, and evaluation protocols will be released soon.

Call for Papers

We invite research papers describing new evaluation methods, datasets, benchmarks, metrics, and analysis of speech and language technologies.

Submissions may include:

  • Novel evaluation methodologies
  • Benchmark datasets and analysis
  • Error analysis of speech models
  • Human evaluation frameworks
  • Evaluation of multilingual and low-resource systems

Accepted papers will be presented as oral or poster presentations during the workshop.
